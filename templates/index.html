The Missing River

Hello, children! Welcome to RadioQuest. I'm your host, Auntie Nia. Today, we have a story from the lush forests of DR Congo. Kofi and Lila stood by the dry riverbed where the mighty Congo River once flowed. The village elders whispered of a time when the river sang with life, but now it was gone, leaving the animals thirsty and the crops wilting. Kofi, with his bright eyes, remembered his grandmother's tales of a hidden spring deep in the forest, guarded by ancient spirits. Lila, always brave, suggested they gather their friends to find it. As they set off, they taught each other about sharing water fairly and keeping the forest clean to protect their home. The path split three ways under the baobab tree. Now, it's your turn to help Kofi and Lila. Where should they go to find the hidden spring? Should they follow the path with animal tracks, climb the hill to ask the wise owl, or cross the old bridge to the misty valley? Think about it, and let us know your choice! This is Auntie Nia, signing off from RadioQuest. Until next time, keep your hearts curious and your minds adventurous!
